Detection Philosophy

Research & Engineering Methodology

Step 01
Threat Research
Monitor CISA KEV, threat intel feeds, vendor advisories, and OSINT. Prioritize actively exploited vulnerabilities and supply chain attacks.
Step 02
TTP Mapping
Map adversary behavior to MITRE ATT&CK. Identify what is behavioral (durable) vs what is infrastructure (transient, rotatable).
Step 03
Rule Engineering
Write detection rules for 10 platforms. Minimum 2 AND-conditions per rule. No hardcoded IPs or domains. Platform field names verified against official documentation.
Step 04
FP Review
Review each rule for false positive risk. Consolidate related detections — max 2 rules per threat per platform. Prefer parent-child process correlation over single-process signals.
Step 05
Publish
Publish HTML report with attack chain visualization, MITRE mapping, IOC table with VirusTotal links, and platform-specific detection rules. Open source, TLP:WHITE.
